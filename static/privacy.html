Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

What Information We Collect

Mikasa Bot collects minimal data required for its functionality on Discord. This includes:

  • Discord User IDs and Usernames.
  • Guild (Server) IDs.
  • Game-related data (Balance, Pokémon, Inventory).
  • Command usage logs for debugging and optimization.

How We Use Your Data

The collected data is strictly used to provide and improve the bot's features, such as maintaining your global economy balance and Pokémon team across different servers.

  • To process game commands and interactions.
  • To prevent fraud, exploiting, and abuse.
  • To improve bot performance and fix bugs.

Data Retention

We store game data as long as the bot is operational or until a user requests deletion. Inactive data may be archived or deleted periodically.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for database management and Render for hosting. We do not sell or share your personal data with any other third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data from our systems. You can do this by contacting the bot developer or joining our support server.
